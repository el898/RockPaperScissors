Game Instructions

How to Play

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors game! Here's how you can play:

  1. Start by choosing one of the three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  2. Once you make your choice, the computer will also make a random selection.
  3. The winner is determined based on the following rules:
    • Rock beats Scissors.
    • Scissors beat Paper.
    • Paper beats Rock.
    • If both you and the computer make the same choice, it's a tie.
  4. Your wins, losses, and ties will be recorded, so try to beat your own score!

Enjoy the Game!

Have fun playing Rock Paper Scissors! It's a simple but entertaining game of chance. See if you can outsmart the computer and achieve the best winning streak.
